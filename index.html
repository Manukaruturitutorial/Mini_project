Cancer Awareness

Breast Cancer

Breast cancer occurs when cells in the breast grow uncontrollably. Regular screening and early detection are important.

Cervical Cancer

Cervical cancer develops in the cervix and is mainly caused by HPV infection. Pap smear tests help early detection.

Early Detection Methods

Clinical Exam

Regular doctor examinations help detect abnormalities early.

Mammography

X-ray imaging used for early detection of breast cancer.

Pap Smear

Test that detects cervical cancer cells early.

Common Symptoms

Lump in breast

Breast pain or swelling
